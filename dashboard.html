Weekly Travel & Market Intel

Chen, Harry, and I visited Indianapolis and Columbus. High absorption of big box and newer traditional industrial product over the last 6 months.

Indianapolis
  • Leasing velocity strong in IOS with rates as high as ~$8,000/acre/mo

Columbus
  • Overbuilt 2020-2023 but vacancy now dropping. Traditional industrial vacancy ~5.5%
  • 2025 net absorption: 11m SF
  • IOS rents around $4,500-6,000
  • Transport, Alterra, Amza most active groups beyond local
