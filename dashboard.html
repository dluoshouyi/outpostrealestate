Weekly Travel & Market Intel

IOS picking up steam; Amazon very active

Seattle
  • Seattle's industrial market picked up significantly entering 2024 after a slow Q4 2023. The most active segments are spaces under 75,000 SF and large-format buildings at 1M+ SF. The mid-range 50K–100K SF multi-tenant space remains slow.
  • AMZ leased or buying $6mm SF in Seattle market (USPS situation)
  • Sea-Tac Airport is highly supply-constrained. Airport-adjacent IOS commands top-of-market rents and should be prioritized.
  • Stabilized industrial and IOS cap rates are reported around 5%–6% going-in, with some solving toward low-mid 6% to ~7%. A B+ building in an A+ location (3-year sale-leaseback) drew ~15 offers and is expected around a 5.25% cap with perceived upside. The lease term "sweet spot" for buyers is ~2–3 years remaining.
  • Many companies here focused on bulk rent too, whereas they are only looking to spend 40-50K/mo
